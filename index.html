This page is my idea on how Ulm should design and build their future tram network.

First of all, we will begin with Hauptbahnhof, the main train station. In my opinion, this should be the central station for the entire tram network.

If you look at Stuttgart, you can see that Hauptbahnhof, and not Charlottenplatz, is where the magic happens.

In Ulm, Ehinger Tor is Charlottenplatz.

Ok, so firstly, the tram station Hauptbahnhof should be rotated by 90 degrees, and should run under the tracks of the train station.
